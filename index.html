About Me

My journey into the world of technology and design has been both rewarding and fulfilling, shaped by a blend of curiosity, creativity, and a passion for continuous learning.

In 2017, I embarked on my first educational venture, pursuing a diploma in advertising. I completed this program with distinction, laying a strong foundation in visual communication and creative thinking. However, my true calling began to crystallize the following year.

In 2018, I made a pivotal decision to dive into the field of IT, driven by my fascination with technology and its limitless possibilities. I enrolled in an IT Technician program, and by early 2020, I had not only completed this program but also expanded my expertise by earning an IT-Informatics degree. Both were completed with distinction, and I was honored with an award for being one of the top achievers in Upper Austria.

But my journey began long before these formal qualifications. Since my teenage years, I have spent countless hours immersed in the world of digital art—tinkering with photos, engaging in photomanipulation, and crafting artwork that speaks to my creative spirit. This passion for visual artistry has always been more than just a hobby; it's a deep-seated love that often kept me working late into the night, unable to pull myself away from a project because I was so captivated by the process.

In 2018, as I faced the crossroads of choosing a professional path, programming emerged as the perfect blend of creativity and intellectual challenge. It was the logical choice—a field where I could create something new while constantly pushing myself to learn and grow. Initially, my motivation to learn was modest, but as I delved deeper into the world of coding, I discovered a love for learning itself. This newfound passion has fueled my commitment to continually expand my knowledge, particularly in areas related to computer science and technology.

Today, I continue to blend my technical skills with my artistic inclinations, constantly exploring new ways to merge these two worlds. Below, you'll find a sneak preview of some of my recent and not-so-recent works—a testament to my ongoing journey and evolution as a coder, artist, and designer.
